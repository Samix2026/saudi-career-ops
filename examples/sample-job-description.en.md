## Senior Operations Manager — KSA & Levant
**[Global Professional Services Firm] | Riyadh Regional Headquarters**

*This role is based at our KSA Regional Headquarters. In line with Saudi Arabia's Regional
Headquarters Program, this position is part of our expanded regional leadership structure
established to serve government and enterprise clients across the Kingdom and Levant region.*

---

### About the Role

We are seeking an experienced Senior Operations Manager to lead day-to-day operational
delivery across our Saudi Arabia and Levant portfolio. Reporting to the Regional COO, you
will own the operational rhythm of a 200+ person professional services delivery team,
drive efficiency initiatives aligned with Saudi Vision 2030 client commitments, and serve
as the primary operational interface with key government and semi-government accounts.

This is a senior individual contributor role with team leadership responsibilities —
you will manage a team of 4 Operations Managers and work closely with Practice Leads,
Finance, HR, and Client Engagement functions.

---

### Key Responsibilities

- Own the end-to-end operational delivery framework for the KSA and Levant region,
  including resource planning, capacity management, and utilization targets
- Drive quarterly operational reviews with Practice Leads and report directly to
  Regional COO on performance against KPIs
- Manage client SLAs for Tier-1 government and semi-government accounts; coordinate
  escalation paths and resolution tracking
- Lead operational transformation initiatives in alignment with the firm's Vision 2030
  localization commitments (Saudization targets, Nitaqat compliance, GOSI reporting)
- Partner with HR to manage workforce planning, including managing a mix of full-time
  employees and contractors under Saudi labor law requirements
- Oversee vendor and procurement relationships, ensuring compliance with Etimad
  portal requirements for government engagements
- Support RHQ licensing obligations and internal compliance reporting to global
  headquarters and Saudi regulatory bodies

---

### Required Qualifications

**Essential:**
- Bachelor's degree in Business Administration, Operations Management, or equivalent;
  MBA preferred
- 10+ years of progressive operations leadership experience, with at least 3 years in
  a senior/regional role
- Demonstrated experience managing multi-functional teams of 150+ across geographically
  distributed locations
- Proven track record delivering operational improvements with measurable KPI impact
  (cost reduction, utilization improvement, SLA adherence)
- Fluent English (working language); functional Arabic preferred but not required
- Prior experience operating in Saudi Arabia or the broader GCC — understanding of
  local labor law, Nitaqat compliance, and government procurement processes is expected

**Preferred:**
- Experience with Etimad portal and government contracting in KSA
- Familiarity with Qiwa platform and Saudi labor contract digitization
- Previous RHQ or regional headquarters operating model experience
- Professional certifications: PMP, LEAN/Six Sigma, or equivalent

- Comprehensive family medical insurance
- Annual flights allowance

- Strong internal mobility opportunities within the global network
